Nuestra Historia

A little corner of Jalisco on Grandview Ave.

Jalisco is the birthplace of so much of what the world calls Mexican food — and it's the heart of everything we cook. At Taquizas Jalisciense, the tortillas are pressed by hand, the al pastor turns on the trompo, and the salsas are made fresh every day the way they are back home.

We're a family taquería and a little Mercado Jalisco all at once: quick, generous plates for a Muscatine lunch, a griddle that runs from morning to evening, and taquizas — taco catering that brings the whole taquería to your celebration.

Tacos al Pastor

Marinated pork off the trompo, sweet with a little pineapple, piled on a warm handmade tortilla with onion and cilantro. The taco Jalisco is famous for — and the one our regulars order by the dozen.

Tacos de Cabeza

Tender, slow-cooked and finished with our creamy avocado salsa — the one people cross town for. Rich, silky and unmistakably Jalisco. If you know, you know.

Tacos de Asada
